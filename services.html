Frequently Asked Questions

At 49Frames, we understand that navigating the world of digital marketing can be complex. To help you get the answers you need, we’ve compiled a list of frequently asked questions. Whether you’re new to digital marketing or looking to enhance your existing strategy, we hope these answers will guide you on your journey to success.

  • What is digital marketing?

    Digital marketing is the use of online platforms, tools, and strategies to promote products, services, or brands. It encompasses various channels such as SEO (Search Engine Optimization), PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising, social media marketing, content marketing, email marketing, and more. The goal is to build an online presence, drive traffic, generate leads, and ultimately, increase sales and conversions.

  • Why do I need digital marketing for my business?

    In today’s digital-first world, having a strong online presence is essential to staying competitive. Digital marketing allows you to reach a wider audience, engage with customers directly, and track the performance of your campaigns in real time. Whether you're a small business or a large enterprise, digital marketing helps you attract new customers, retain existing ones, and boost revenue.

  • What services does 49 Frames offer?

    Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Improving your website’s visibility in search engine results. Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising: Running paid ad campaigns to drive targeted traffic to your site. Social Media Marketing: Managing and growing your social media presence to engage with customers. Content Marketing: Creating valuable content that attracts and retains customers. Web Design & Development: Designing and developing responsive, user-friendly websites that drive conversions. Email Marketing: Creating email campaigns to nurture leads and build customer relationships. Digital Strategy & Consulting*: Tailored strategies to align your digital efforts with business goals.

  • How do I know which digital marketing services I need?

    Every business is unique, and so are its needs. We begin by understanding your business goals, target audience, and current digital presence. Based on that, we create a customized digital marketing strategy that incorporates the services that will drive the best results for your business. Whether you need to boost brand awareness, generate leads, or increase sales, our team will help determine the right mix of services.

  • How long does it take to see results from SEO?

    SEO is a long-term strategy, and while the results vary based on factors like industry competitiveness and the current state of your website, typically, it can take 3 to 6 months to start seeing significant improvements in search rankings and organic traffic. However, SEO provides lasting benefits, helping you build a sustainable online presence over time.
